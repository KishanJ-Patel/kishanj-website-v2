Somewhere in Space
Download Android APK
Prepare for an adrenaline-fueled journey in "Somewhere in Space," a captivating 3D space shooter game. As the sole developer of this project, I have utilized Unity and C# to create an immersive and action-packed gaming experience.
In "Somewhere in Space," players find themselves in the depths of the cosmos armed with a powerful gun. Your objective is to eliminate wandering spheres that pose a threat to your mission. While movement is limited, players can rotate their viewpoint to target and unleash a barrage of shots upon the spheres.
The game delivers an intense and addictive gameplay experience, with a focus on precise aiming, reflexes, and strategic decision-making. With its minimalist design, the game allows players to concentrate solely on the thrilling sphere-blasting action.
On this portfolio page, you will find captivating screenshots and videos that showcase the immersive 3D environment, dynamic sphere behavior, and visually striking effects of "Somewhere in Space."
As the developer, I meticulously crafted the gameplay mechanics, designed the captivating visuals, and implemented engaging sound effects. Leveraging my expertise in Unity and C#, I ensured seamless controls, captivating graphics, and a challenging gameplay experience.
"Somewhere in Space" represents my ability to create immersive and intense gaming experiences, even within limited movement mechanics. It highlights my attention to detail, problem-solving skills, and dedication to delivering an engaging player experience.
